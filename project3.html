Goodnight Ramen   (๑ᵔ⤙ᵔ๑)

A cherished childhood memory I aimed to incorporate was enjoying mum's delicious cooking and being cozy indoors whilst watching the rain outside. But on the contrary, I also vividly remember having an underlying fear of night rain, especially when I was alone without my family by my side. Hence my ‘goodnight ramen environment is a combination of both memories, a transition between positive and negative emotions. my mum's homemade cooking and bunny toy, comforts me against my fears of the dark. I wanted to show the ending of the transformation as a beautiful garden wonderland as a reward of my courage to face my fears.

Sound in my environment was split into three main parts for the three different map scenes. The ambiance added depth and character to the scenes Although I did not have time to create additional sound effects, if given the chance, I would definitely add more environmental effects such as door closing, wind rustling, footsteps and even birds chirping.


Different elements were unified through the use of colour and style - realistic yet childlike


Play testing was important in this environment as it assisted me in realising the use of captions and instructional guides. When offered friends the free reign to play the game without any information, they were confused at the aim of the game, and the direction of the player - some couldn’t even find the pathway to the different environments which prevented the aim of the game. Upon adding on-screen text prompts and little informational notes, users were able to navigate the environment and play the game to my desired outcome - reaching the end environment of the peaceful garden.
