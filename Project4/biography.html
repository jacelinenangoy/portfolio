A little about me,,



Hello!

Nice to meet you :)

My name is Jaceline Nangoy, a first year student studying the Bachelor of Design (Digital Media) at RMIT.

Having loved art and technology as a child, I intend to explore the best of both worlds and look into how user interactions influence design in the present and future.

I am interested in the process of visualising ideas and implementing them into reality.

Apart from design, I am also a big foodie, coffee enthusiast and avid baker. Whether it be discovering a new hidden cafe to trying a new recipe at home, you can count on me to research and appreciate something new each time!

Growing up in Perth and recently moving to Melbourne last year, everyday is a new adventure and I adore the change of pace.

Although I am an introvert and love my personal time to recharge and unwind, I'd love to get to know new people, so please don't hesitate in getting in touch over some design related enquiries, cafe recommendations, or even just to have a chat!

- Jace
